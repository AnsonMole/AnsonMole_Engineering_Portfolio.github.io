BETA Technologies (2024-2025)

I completed two consecutive six-month co-ops supporting electric VTOL aircraft development. On the Lift Kit team, I designed test equipment and manufacturing systems for prototype propeller production. I later joined the Advanced Concepts team, where I designed and built a 3,000 cubic foot production-scale composites oven. This role expanded my experience in thermal system design, industrial controls, and developing manufacturing processes to meet aerospace standards.

Novatio Engineering (2023)

Novatio was my first engineering co-op, where I optimized power output and efficiency of military jet fuel generators through air-to-fuel ratio optimization. This experience introduced me combustion systems and refined my trouble shooting skills for mechanical and electrical systems.
