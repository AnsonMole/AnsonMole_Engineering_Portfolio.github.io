About Me

I'm a mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University graduating in Spring 2026. I'm passionate about aerospace, robotics, and developing innovative solutions to complex engineering challenges.

Professional Journey

My engineering journey began in high school robotics, which sparked my interest in mechanical design and problem-solving. Through Northeastern's co-op program, I've gained extensive hands-on experience at leading aerospace companies:

BETA Technologies (2024-2025)

Completed two consecutive 6-month co-ops working on electric VTOL aircraft development. Started with the Lift Kit team designing test equipment and manufacturing systems for prototype propellers, then moved to the Advanced Concepts team where I led the development of a production-scale composites oven.

Novatio Engineering (2023)

My first engineering co-op, where I optimized power output and efficiency of military jet fuel generators through air-to-fuel ratio optimization. This experience introduced me to aerospace applications and thermal systems engineering.

Beyond Engineering

Outside of work, I'm an avid backcountry skier exploring Vermont's mountains and maintain a consistent training regimen focused on building functional strength. I believe that diverse experiences inform better engineering solutions.

Looking Forward

As I complete my final semester, I'm seeking opportunities in aerospace R&D where I can contribute to cutting-edge projects in a fast-paced, creative environment. I thrive on solving difficult problems and working with teams that push boundaries.
